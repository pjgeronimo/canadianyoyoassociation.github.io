About Canadian Nationals 2025

Welcome to the official website of the Canadian National Return Top Championship 2025! Some of the finest return top players from all over Canada will be competing for this year's nationals titles in Toronto on Saturday July 5. As a member organization of the International Yo-Yo Federation (IYYF), the winners of Canadian Nationals will be seeded to the World YoYo Contest 2025.

This event is family-friendly and open to the public for free. Spectators may enter this event without admission fees. Contestants must register here.

The social media hashtag is #CanadaNats2025. To receive the latest updates or get in touch with us, make sure to follow CANYA on social media.
